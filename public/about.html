What is this all about?

This website is being presented through the backend.
Which means:

  • I didn't use a framework
  • I got a look at how routing works behind the scenes
  • I got a look at how files are actually read and served

That's cool, is this going to be some part of a portfolio?

Yes and no. I am actually starting to enjoy this. I like building new stuff and tinker with it.
A slow road set in stone, so it can weather the time.
